Frequently Asked Questions

What materials do you use in your furniture?

We use a variety of materials in our furniture, including wood, metal, glass, and fabric. Each piece is designed with quality and durability in mind, so you can enjoy it for years to come.

How do I care for my furniture?

The care of your furniture depends on the material. For wood furniture, avoid direct sunlight and use a soft cloth to dust regularly. For metal furniture, wipe with a damp cloth and dry immediately to prevent rust. For fabric furniture, vacuum regularly and treat stains promptly. For more specific care instructions, consult the manufacturer's recommendations.
